ABOUT US

Levert Interiors is a full service interior design company located in th Abu- Dhabi, specializing in both residential and commercial design.

We are ISO registered company, started by team of professionals with over 30 years of collective experience in Interior fit-out market in U.A.E. We have provided Turn-key solutions from medium to large companies, including multinational, corporates and oil companies in Dubai and Abu-Dhabi.

We have achieved our success through our focus to understand clearly the client’s business, their business model, their aspirations, and above all their aesthetics and functional requirements for Offices,retail space, health care, Hospitality space and Institutional spaces.
